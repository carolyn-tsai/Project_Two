Word Cloud Visualization

A representation of data through text

A Word Cloud is a visual representation of text data in the form of tags. Tags are typically single words or short phrases whose importance or frequency is visualized by way of their size and color. The more frequent a tag occurs in the dataset, the bigger and bolder the word or phrase.

Analysis of Our Data

We created three word cloud visualizations based on our data. Each word cloud shows the frequency of restaurants by neighborhood in the San Francisco area.

  • 1. Dog Friendly Restaurants
  • 2. Wheelchair Accessible Restaurants
  • 3. Restaurants with Both
